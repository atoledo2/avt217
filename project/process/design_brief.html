Design Brief for Portfolio Website

My goal is to make a site to showcase by work and give some information about me for employers or interested parties.

Design

I want my portoflio site to be easy on the eyes and overall simple to navigate. To do this I can:

Use light colors such as pastels or off-whites for the background
Have navigation buttons on the header under my name or logo
Have a back to homepage button or link at the end of every page
Divide all my work into categories
